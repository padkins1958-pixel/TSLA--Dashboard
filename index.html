📌 THE GOLDEN RULE
Monitor all hours for awareness. Only act on calls during green WATCH windows when Quick Glance shows 3/4 or 4/4. NEVER hold calls overnight.
📖 WHAT DO GAP UP AND GAP DOWN MEAN?
🚀 GAP UP — TSLA opens HIGHER than it closed yesterday. Example: closed at $403, opens at $407. That $4 difference is the gap. Means overnight buyers were aggressive. Generally bullish for calls — but wait for 9:45 AM to confirm it holds before buying. Sometimes the gap fills back down in the first 30 minutes.
📉 GAP DOWN — TSLA opens LOWER than yesterday's close. Example: closed at $405, opens at $401. Bad overnight news or selling pressure. Cautious with calls — wait for a clear reversal signal. Watch the 2:00-2:30 PM low point for a potential bounce setup.
➡️ FLAT OPEN — Opens within $1.50 of yesterday's close. No strong overnight opinion. Let the first 30 minutes set direction before making any decisions. Your signals will tell you which way it wants to go.
